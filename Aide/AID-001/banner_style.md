### üîß Implementation Notes

- **Font stack**: Liberation Sans (Bold for title), Liberation Mono (for code/paths)
- **Grid spacing**: 30-40px intervals for subtle background pattern
- **Icon sizing**: Scale proportionally to maintain readability at both dimensions
- **Hash truncation**: Use ellipsis (`...`) to suggest full hash without cluttering
- **Arrow styling**: 2-3px width, solid cyan with polygon arrowheads
- **Database ellipse**: Layered horizontal ellipses create 3D cylinder effect
- **File count**: 3-4 files for wide format, 2-3 for compact version
